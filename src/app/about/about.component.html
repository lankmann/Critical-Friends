Who Could Benefit from Critical Friends' Services?

  • Organizations and leaders who recognize the advantage of fostering an inclusive environment.
  • Organizations and leaders that have begun the journey to make themselves more inclusive and equitable.
  • Organizations and leaders that have a desire to continue to challenge themselves and make DEI a core institutional value.
  • Organizations and leaders that desire to be diverse in all three areas: Color, Culture and Consciousness.
